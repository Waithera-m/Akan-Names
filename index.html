Akan Naming Customs

Naming customs vary across the continent, and the Akan people of Ghana arguably have one of the most fascinating ways of assigning names. Unlike other cultural groups where a child or adult's names is determined by the time of day they were born or their ancestors, the Akan people use weekdays.

Thus, the first name is a reflection of the day an individual was born. This name also offers useful glimpses into a person's soul and character. So in this culture, you can judge a person by their name.
