Akan Naming Customs

Naming customs vary across the continent, and the Akan people of Ghana arguably have one of the most fascinating ways of assigning names. Unlike other cultural groups where a child or adult's names is determined by their time of day or ancestors, the Akan poeple use weekdays

Thus, the your first name is a reflection of the day your were born. In addition to indicating the day an individual was born, day names also offer useful glimpses into the soul and character of their bearers. So in this case you can judge a person
